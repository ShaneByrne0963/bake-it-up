About our Local Dublin Bakery

Bake It Up is a bakery based in the heart of Dublin city centre. We offer a wide variety of personalised bread, pastries and cakes for you to sink your teeth into.

At Bake It Up, customization is key, where we actively encourage all our patrons to unleash their creativity. Maybe today you feel like having a red velvet, white chocolate chip cookie, but tomorrow a buttercream filled cupcake with peanut butter icing could really hit the spot. Perhaps you have a friend's birthday coming up, and you're thinking of getting them a birthday cake with a custom message, or you are looking to order a wedding cake for your special day. Here, the power is in your hands to create something beautiful, and we're happy to bring your creations to life!

With Bake It Up, you now have the option to order your bread and pastries online. We also give the opportunity to deliver straight to your doorstep, anywhere in the Republic of Ireland! That means you can get your hands on our fresh treats without having to leave the comfort of your own home!

How We Began

Bake It Up was founded by friends Liam Dowd and Evan McManus. The pair shared a passion for experimenting with different kinds of desserts, constantly throwing new ideas at the baking table. They spent the early part of their baking careers jotting down what worked, and learning from what didn't.

Dowd and McManus continued to gather experience working in several different bakeries throughout Dublin, up until 2012, when they decided on opening a small bakery called Bake It Up. In it's inception, the bakery had a small, but loyal, following. People familiar with the place returned on the regular, looking for their fill of the sweet treats they grew familiar with. Word of mouth spread, and Bake It Up became a common name in the streets of Dublin.

Bake It Up became so successful that Dowd and McManus had to move to a bigger location, to accomodate the needs of its growing fan base. The bakery was moved to Wood Quay, where it continues to satisfy the sugar cravings of its happy pastryholics!
